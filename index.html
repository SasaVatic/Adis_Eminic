Songwriter & Producer

About

I started my career in broadcasting at the local Bay Area radio station, 96.1 FM. Upon entering the radio world I worked as an intern in the international programming department. With time, I inherited a lot of responsibilities and challenges becoming junior producer/writer of a nightly talk show. Eventually, I graduated to a programming director developing a very successful nightly “countdown” show. Our rich international community tuning in, allowed us to focus on global market introducing our listeners to new music via on air and live programming.

The show received governor’s awards for excellence in broadcasting three years in a row.

Upon my departure from the radio, I entered the publishing world working as a creative director for Crowned Ark Publishing. It focused on booking shows for various artists and performers, provided representation either solely or throughout our extended and very experienced affiliates, managed projects (including marketing campaigns), and published material for American and European markets. Additionally, I consulted various agencies to fight music pirating to build stability within the music industry in the Balkan region. I maintain a supporting relationship with EESA (Eastern European Service Agency) providing services that benefit the well-being of the local community. Besides their vast media involvement; they also focus on elderly care, child well-being, and mental health of which I am currently a contributor.

As a songwriter I have published material for the United States and European markets.

Presently, I have a startup with an American based multi-platinum producer, Bryan Todd (Jordin Sparks, Miley Cyrus, Ashley Tisdale, Big Time Rush to name a few) at Universal Music Studios where I currently serve as a managing director. 2220 Records is a recording media company with international production and distribution.
